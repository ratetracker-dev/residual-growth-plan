Questions, answered

Is it really free?

Yes. The Residual Growth Plan is free to build and yours to download. There's no credit card and no obligation. It's a resource for the payments community.

Do I have to be a Rate Tracker partner to use it?

No. Anyone in the payments industry can build a plan, whether you're already with us or not. If you like what you see and want to go further, partnering is one option, but it's never required.

How long does it take?

About three minutes. You answer a short set of questions about your goals and prospecting channels, and your plan generates automatically.

How do I log in?

Enter your email and we send you a secure one-tap login link. No password to remember. Open the link from your inbox and you're in.

Where do the numbers come from?

The plan uses real portfolio economics from the payments business, including typical residual per account and close rates, then scales the activity to match the goal you set.
